Capabilities
Versatile, Reliable, and Expert Technology Services

Cloud Migration

We help government agencies with their cloud computing needs, including migration of applications, with a flawless, multi-tiered process that is shaped from the Office of Management Budget’s (OMB) cloud-first policy, FedRAMP guidelines, and National Institute of Standard and Technology (NIST) approved cloud computing security architecture. It enables agencies to reduce costs by paying only for computing resources they use. Our expert cloud migration services enable smooth migration of mission-critical applications to scalable cloud environments with deliberate stages including strategize and assess to develop a clear roadmap, proof of concept, data and application migration, and tune in to optimize and enhance performance. We are also proficient in delivering hybrid cloud migration, which we undertake when the associated environment comprises two or more independent cloud environments (private and public).

Bioinformatics

Our service capability for bioinformatics is as deep as it is sophisticated. With the help of our expert team of bioinformaticians, statisticians, molecular biologists, computer scientists, and scientific programmers, we provide in-depth bioinformatics analysis to provide high-quality, publication-ready genomics and proteomics data, including Next Generation Sequence data management and analysis, Genotyping and SNP data analysis, Microarray data analysis and tools, structural and functional genomics, and statistic and bio-mathematical modeling.

Program Management

By means of our program management services embedded with industry best practices and transparent processes, government agencies are getting high-quality healthcare, and other, initiatives, built from concept to release to allow friction-less adoption. Our principled and client-oriented project management practices, employing agile methodology, and best practices surrounding it, allow us to deliver custom solutions suitable for the environment. Our trained resources and strategies allow smooth rendition of complex programs based on useable pilot results.

Regulatory Compliance & Auditability Assessment

There are endless regulatory compliance related issues that agencies have to worry about while adapting to new procedures and technologies. Our expert team of compliance specialists is always on-board in our projects to ensure the services we provide and the systems we develop for our clients stay within the compliance limits set by agencies, such as NIST. We ensure clients’ sophisticated software, research, projects, programs, and data are well within the regulations our clients are expected to follow.
We also assess client agencies’ current financial statements and related processes particularly in relation to transparent audits. Our other services in this domain include development of audit milestones specifically defined for every task, assistance in improving quality of system data for audits, identification of alternate pathways for presenting ledger balances without supporting data, enhancement of data related processes, and delivery of recommendations to meet Statement of Federal Financial Accounting Standards’ compliance requirements.

Data Analytics

In the data economy of the 21st century, we are helping federal agencies optimize their data analytics potential with sound strategies, diverse tools, and reliable forecasting of trends. Instead of giving our clients a one-off expensive solution, we build them a data analytics capability that allows flexibility, enterprise approach, and mission-focused insights. For ever-increasing customer expectations, we help government agencies build automation analytics for personalized services and tailored solutions.

Software Development

When it comes to software development, we help federal agencies not only in developing software with best-in-line features but also choosing the technologies they should adopt. We build software applications on a range of platforms to meet the specific needs of our clients so they can deliver flawless services to their customers. We also re-factor key components of systems to improve performance, functionality and reliability. To enable smooth transformation towards shortening of release cycle times, we have adopted the DevOps approach, which emphasizes on collaboration between software development and operations teams.
