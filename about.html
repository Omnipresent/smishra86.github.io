About Us
Innovation-driven. Customer-centric. Solution-oriented

Mission

As frontline proponents of innovation and process improvement, we aim to deliver expert guidance, and assistance, to government agencies for a wide range of exemplary technology services. Our diverse team members - each a subject matter expert in their own field - converge to our business mission and vision with the sole ambition to help federal government agencies achieve faster, more effective, and cost-effective operations through a number of project based services, such as flawless cloud migration, extensive scientific research, superior software development, demonstrable task progress with agile frameworks, and expert project management.

Values

While we make for a highly ambitious team, our decisions and practices are all rooted in our core values, which include:

  • Strategy

    We understand the philosophy of our clients before developing solutions. We believe principles outlive tactics ― while tactics provide the "what" and "how"" we focus on the "why" for sustainable solutions and lasting results.

  • Adaptability

    We are a growing outfit. We are lean and fast. Our leadership and management include seasoned program managers, scientists, attorneys, and patented inventors. Our budding growth allows us to be flexible for our clients, innovate faster, ensure higher ROI for our clients, and dedicate extraordinary attention to their projects.

  • Simplicity

    Our professional practices are rooted into our philosophy of simplicity. We believe in Occam’s razor and avoid creating overly complex solutions to our clients’ business problems, which allows us to deliver the most user-friendly designs and solutions.

  • Humanity

    We approach projects with focus on the humans involved. We see people, not users. Our solutions are human-centric and developed to make people’s lives easier and to enable them to make smarter, more meaningful, and lasting decisions.

  • Trust

    Our processes and practices are built to solicit clients’ trust. We strongly believe this is the cornerstone of our growth and success, since our transparency, communication, and openness forge bountiful relationships between us and our clients.

Leadership

Shubhi Mishra J.D., M.S. (CEO): Ms. Mishra has many years of experience overseeing multi-million-dollar IDIQs. BPAs, and Task Orders. She has extensive experience in helping public and private organizations achieve their business objectives. She blends deep knowledge of technology with thorough understanding of collaborative research to develop and deploy solutions that integrate bioinformatics with traditional IT in supporting collaborative research business processes. She has over decade’s worth of experience assisting federal government agencies as a technology and organization consultant to effectively manage change, adopt innovative technology, and improve processes. She is also a published author of scientific papers. Ms. Mishra received her Masters in Bioinformatics from Boston University and her Juris Doctorate from George Washington University.

Quality

When it comes to delivering superior quality services in any of the domains we approach together, our clients know we have them covered.
